# Ingredients
### Main
- 1 kohlrabi
- 1 Carrot
- 50g Peanuts
- Thai basil
- Mint
- Coriander
- Garlic
- Lime
- Chili
- Vinegar
- Sugar
- MSG
- Fish sauce
- Salt

# Procedure
### Main
- Peel kohlrabi, get rid of the stiffer and harder parts and especially the stem area containing lots of fiber
- Use big knife, cut kohlrabi into small stripes
- Put the cut kohlrabi into a bowl, sprinkle 2 tbsp salt on top and let it soak for 1hr. This extracts water from the kohlrabi, making it more crunchy. Squeeze it to get rid of water
- Cut half of the basil, mint, coriander; keep rest as is
- Roast peanuts: Turn on stove, add some salt to a pan. After a minute, add peanuts for short time. Turn off heat, let peanuts cool, crush peanuts.
- Peel carrot, cut into small stripes
- Add 1/2 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, mix, let soak for 15min making it crispy
- Squeeze to get rid of water
- Add 5 tsp vinegar and let soak for as long as possible
- Dry the kohlrabi and carrot in a dry cloth
- Put kohlrabi, carrot, basil, coriander, mint together

### Sauce
- Peel, crush, cut onion. Cut chili
- Mix: 1 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp lime, 1/2 tbsp vinegar, chili, garlic
- Add 3 tsp fish sauce, 3 tsp water. Mix well
- Taste test

### Mix
- Drip sauce evenly over the salad. Mix well (with hand). Let sauce soak for 15 min

### Garnish
- Salad on a plate, sprinkle peanuts, basil, mint and coriander on top
