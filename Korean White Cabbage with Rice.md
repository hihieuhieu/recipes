# Ingredients:
### Main:
- White cabbage
- Jasmin rice

### Sauce:
- 5 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp white vinegar
- 1 tbsp korean chili powder
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 green onion, chopped
- 1/2 chili, chopped
- Sesame seeds

# Procedure:
### Mains:
- Cook rice
- Steam cabbage, individual leafes for approx. 10 min

### Sauce:
- Mix soy sauce, vinegar, chili powder, sesame oil, green onions, sesame, chili

# Eat:
- Wrap rice and sauce into cabbage leaf, eat
