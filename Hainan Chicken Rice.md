# Ingredients
### Main
#### Chicken
- Whole chicken (Maishähnchen!!!!!!)
- 5 cm ginger, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 stalk scallion
- 1 tbsp salt
- water
- (ice), (coriander leaves)

#### Rice
- 250 g jasmin rice
- 1 tbsp chicken fat / oil
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 leaves Pandan
- 350 ml chicken broth

### Sauce
#### Sauce: Chili sauce
- 6-9 red chili
- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- 5 cm ginger, grated
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp lime juice
- 80 ml chicken broth

#### Ginger Garlic sauce
- 90g ginger, grated
- 30g garlic, grated
- 20 ml chicken broth
- 1/4 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp chicken broth

#### Soy sauce
- 2 tbsp light soy sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp chicken broth

### Soup
- cucumber, slices
- 400 ml chicken broth
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp ground white pepper

# Procedure
### Main
#### Chicken
- Fill pot with water covering entire chicken
- Clean chicken skin with salt, then with running water
- Add ginger, garlic, scallion to pot or inside chicken
- Put chicken into pot with water, bring to a boil
- Simmer chicken for 20 minutes at boiling temperature
- Switch off stove, let chicken continue cooking for another twenty minutes
- Take off chicken, soak in (ice) cold water until cold
- Cut chicken
- Garnish with scallion, coriander leaves
- Optional, but massive taste enhancer: Put chicken+sauce into fridge for some hrs/overnight

#### Rice
- Sauté garlic and ginger with chicken fat in a pan
- Wash rice, add rice to pan and stir fry for a minute
- Put rice mix into rice cooker, add pandan leave

#### Sauce
- Blend/mix ingredients together

# Eat
- Plate with rice, chicken aside with sauce dripped on the top, cucumber and chick broth
