# Ingredients
### Main
- 3 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 7 cloves garlic, sliced
- 3 Thai bird chilies
- 450g ground pork
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp fish sauce
- 1 tsp light soy sauce
- 2 tsp dark soy sauce
- 2 tsp oyster sauce
- 1/3 cup chicken broth or water
- 1,5 cups packed holy basil leaves
- jasmin rice

# Procedure
### Main
- wok on medium high heat
- add oil, shallots, garlic, fry for 3 min
- add chilies for another min
- heat up to highest, add ground pork, fry until crispy
- add sugar, fish sauce, soy sauces, oyster sauce, stir for a minute
- deglaze pan with broth
- turn off heat
- use remaining heat, add holy basil leaves. dont let the leaves burn, it will release unpleasant taste

# Eat
- Eat with rice
